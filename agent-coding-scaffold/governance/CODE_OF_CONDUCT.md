# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and constructive community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in technical disagreements.
- Focus on issues, proposals, and evidence.
- Assume good intent and ask for clarification.
- Give actionable feedback, not personal attacks.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- Personal attacks or abusive language
- Deliberate disruption of collaboration

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may warn, moderate, or remove participants who violate this policy.
