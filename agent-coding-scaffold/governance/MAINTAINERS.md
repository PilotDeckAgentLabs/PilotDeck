## Maintainer responsibilities

- Keep docs, templates, and schemas consistent
- Enforce release process and changelog quality
- Review compatibility impact on every normative change
- Ensure issue triage and contributor response health

## Becoming a maintainer

- Demonstrated high-quality contributions
- Familiarity with scaffold core artifacts (`.agent` templates + schemas)
- Maintainer consensus approval

## Inactivity policy

Maintainers with no project activity for an extended period may be moved to emeritus status by consensus.
